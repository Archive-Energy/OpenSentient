# Sensemaking

The retrospective layer of the Alethic Method.

Individual position updates are data points. Sensemaking asks
what they mean together — what pattern is emerging, what the
domain is becoming, what the Sentient now knows that it didn't.

## Approach
Weick's sensemaking adapted for domain intelligence.
Retrospective: make sense of what just happened.
Plausible: not certain, but the best available narrative.
Enacted: the act of making sense changes what is noticed next.

## Process
1. List all positions updated this session
2. List all new inquiries opened
3. Identify the pattern: what do these updates have in common?
4. State the domain trajectory: where is this domain moving?
5. Identify watch signals: what would confirm or deny the pattern?
6. Write session narrative to record/:id.md
7. Update INDEX.md ## Domain Trajectory section

## Output
session_pattern
domain_trajectory
watch_signals[]
session_narrative (written to record/:id.md)

## Alethic Role
Transforms individual uncoverings into domain understanding.
The Sentient doesn't just accumulate positions — it develops
a theory of where its domain is going.
